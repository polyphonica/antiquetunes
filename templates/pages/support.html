Downloads

Where do I find my downloads after purchase?

If you have an account, your downloads are permanently available in your Downloads Library. If you checked out as a guest, download links are included in your order confirmation email.

How many times can I download a file?

Account holders have unlimited downloads with no expiry. Guest download links expire after 30 days and allow up to 5 downloads per file. We recommend creating a free account to keep permanent access.

My download link has expired. What do I do?

Email us at support@antiquetunes.com with your order reference number and we'll send you a fresh link.

What format are the files?

All sheet music is delivered as a high-resolution PDF, typically scanned at 300 dpi or above from original early 20th century copies.

Can I print the sheet music?

Yes. You may print the PDF for your own personal, non-commercial use. Please see our Terms of Service for full details.

Orders & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) via Stripe's secure checkout. Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported where available.

I didn't receive a confirmation email. What should I do?

Check your spam or junk folder first. If it's not there, email us at support@antiquetunes.com with the email address you used at checkout and we'll look into it.

Can I get a refund?

Because our products are digital downloads that are accessible immediately after purchase, we do not offer refunds as a matter of course. However, if you have a genuine issue — for example the file is corrupt or not as described — please contact us and we'll make it right.

Where can I find my order reference?

Your order reference (e.g. AT-2025-00042) is shown on your order confirmation email and in your Order History if you have an account.

Account

Do I need an account to buy?

No — you can check out as a guest. However, creating a free account gives you permanent, unlimited access to all your downloads in one place.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the Login page, click "Forgot your password?" and we'll email you a reset link.

I bought something as a guest. Can I link it to my account?

Yes. If you register with the same email address used at checkout, your previous orders and downloads will be merged into your account automatically.

Content & Copyright

Is this music in the public domain?

The underlying musical compositions in our catalogue — generally published before 1928 — are in the public domain in the United States. Our high-resolution scans and digitised editions represent our own original work and are protected by copyright.

Can I use the sheet music in a performance or recording?

You may perform and record the underlying compositions freely, as they are in the public domain. You may not reproduce, resell, or redistribute our PDF editions. See our Terms of Service for full details.
